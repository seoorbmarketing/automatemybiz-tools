Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy applies to all browser extensions, online tools, and services provided by AutomateMyBiz ("we", "our", or "us").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 For All Tools

  • Email address (for authenticated users)
  • Usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Error logs for debugging

1.2 Product-Specific Data

AI Commenting Sidekick Chrome Extension:

  • Screenshots: Processed locally and immediately deleted after generating response
  • Generated responses: Stored temporarily for session continuity
  • Profile settings: Stored locally in your browser
  • Credit usage: Tracked for billing purposes

Free Online Tools:

  • Input data: Processed in real-time, not stored
  • Generated outputs: Not stored on our servers

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To communicate important updates
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • User data is stored on secure Supabase servers
  • Payment information is processed by Stripe and never stored on our servers
  • We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and data storage
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • OpenAI: For AI text generation (no user data is stored by OpenAI)
  • Vercel: For hosting our services

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies only for:

  • User preferences

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
